Mirelurk cakes

Description

A cake made from Mirelurk meat.

Provides +140 hit points and allows breathing underwater for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Mirelurk egg
  • Mirelurk meat
  • Oil
  • Razorgrain

Steps

  1. Find a Mirelurk egg in a Mirelurk nest
  2. Find Mirelurk meat on a dead Mirelurk
  3. Acquire oil
    • Find oil in the wasteland
    • Scrap objects at a workshop
  4. Find Razorgrain in the wasteland
  5. Cook at a cooking station
