Iguana-on-a-stick

Description

The flesh of a small lizard, which has been baked.

Provides +40 hit points.

Ingredients

  • Iguana bits (1)
  • Wood (1)

Steps

  1. Acquire iguana bits
    • Find on the bodies of the unalive
    • Find scattered throughout the wasteland
    • Purchase from vendors
  2. Acquire wood
    • Scrap miscellaneous objects at a workshop
  3. Cook at a cooking station
